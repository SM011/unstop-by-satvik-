All that you need to know about Venture Vendetta | Business Simulation Competition

“As long as there is money to be made from wars there will always be wars”

It’s not just a crisis, but a war! Gear up with your weapons of business acumen, strategic thinking, quick decision-making, negotiations and tactics to fight your way forward. Get ready for a thrilling and exhilarating competition with a wholesome, fun and learning experience.

The Finance & Investment Cell of Ramjas College brings to you Venture Vendetta; a one of its kind Business Simulation Competition which will put forth such situations and circumstances that will unleash your inner businessman! Do you have what it takes to go through 3 intellectually challenging rounds full of war, drama, crisis, and betrayal or will you collapse under pressure?

Register now!

Guidelines:

  • Cross-college teams are allowed
  • Team size: 2-4
  • All rounds will be held online
  • Each round is eliminative in nature
  • The organizing team’s decision will be final and binding
